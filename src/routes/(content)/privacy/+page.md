# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-18_

Vryne is an energy-grid simulation game. We collect as little personal data as
possible.

## What we store

- **Account data**: When you sign in with Google or Facebook we receive your
  name and email address from the provider and store them to identify your
  account. We never receive or store your password.
- **Nickname**: The public handle you choose. It is visible to other players
  on the leaderboard.
- **Game data**: Your grid state (buildings, resources, experience points),
  which is linked to your account.
- **Session data**: A session cookie, your IP address, and user agent, used
  solely to keep you signed in and to secure your account.

## What we do NOT do

- No advertising, no tracking pixels, no analytics profiles.
- No sale or sharing of your data with third parties.
- No posting to your social accounts and no access to your contacts.

## Third-party sign-in

Authentication is performed by Google and/or Facebook. Their privacy policies
apply to the sign-in step:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Facebook Data Policy](https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy)

## External services

To simulate your grid we fetch **regional weather data** (Open-Meteo) and
**public electricity spot prices**. These requests are made by our server and
contain no personal data.

## Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any
time. Deleting your account removes your user record, sessions, and game
state.

## Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact the operator listed in the
